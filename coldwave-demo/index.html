Interpretation

How to read this panel

  • Cold-Wave Score: higher = more resilient.
  • Hybrid score: 0.5 * potential resilience + 0.5 * observed resilience.
  • Potential-only: Tier 1/2 potential resilience only.
  • Evidence level: direct NPMRDS observed support status.
  • Observed curve: available only with direct NPMRDS support.
