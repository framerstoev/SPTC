Metric dictionary

  • EVENT_REI: Event-specific screening score for the selected hazard-window.
  • AADT: Annual Average Daily Traffic.
  • WEATHER_REI: Weather-driven exposure score adjusted by surface type.
  • NETRISK_LITE: Traffic-weighted network risk (consequence + redundancy).
  • CONSEQ_LITE: Local structural importance and traffic consequence proxy.
  • REDUND_LITE: Availability of alternative routes in a 1 km neighborhood.
